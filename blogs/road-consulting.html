Pavement Failure Analysis

Every failure mode tells a story about loads, layers and water. We investigate the surface, the structure and the history — not just patch what is visible.

Cracking

Fatigue, block and reflective cracking caused by repeated loading, layer weakness or movement below the surface.

Rutting

Channelised wheel-path deformation due to heavy traffic, weak layers or material instability.

Settlement

Subgrade or fill consolidation that causes differential movement, uneven pavement and long-term service issues.

Potholes

Localised structural breakdown, often accelerated by water entry and repeated wheel loading.

Surface deformation

Shoving, corrugation and heaving caused by braking, turning, material issues or poor confinement.

Water damage

Stripping, pumping, poor drainage and moisture-induced layer deterioration during monsoon cycles.
